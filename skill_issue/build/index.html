Welcome to Skill Issue

We are a non-profit organisation that works towards helping the paralysed and specially abled people in using electronic devices. Currently our works are proof of concepts, however, on scaling this can lead to improvement of many lives.

Currently we have developed two types of gesture monitoring namely the eye-tracking and hand gesture tracking to move the cursor and interact with it.
